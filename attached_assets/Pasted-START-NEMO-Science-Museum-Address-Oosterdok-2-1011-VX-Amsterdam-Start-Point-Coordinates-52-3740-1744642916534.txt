START: NEMO Science Museum
Address: Oosterdok 2, 1011 VX Amsterdam

Start Point Coordinates: 52.37403, 4.91243

Step 1: Walk from NEMO to Montelbaanstoren
Exit NEMO, walk west across the pedestrian bridge (Mr. J.J. van der Veldebrug).

Continue straight along Oosterdokskade, which curves left into Oudeschans.

Walk south along the canal until you reach the Montelbaanstoren.

Stop Coordinates: 52.37139, 4.90870

Step 2: Walk to Nieuwmarkt & In de Waag
Continue west on Oudeschans, which becomes Sint Antoniesbreestraat.

Walk straight through the small square past shops and restaurants.

Enter Nieuwmarkt Square.

Destination building: In de Waag (central castle-like building).

Stop Coordinates: 52.37284, 4.90146

Step 3: Walk up Zeedijk to He Hua Temple
Exit Nieuwmarkt north along Zeedijk street (main Chinatown street).

Walk 150 meters to Fo Guang Shan He Hua Temple, on your left.

Stop Coordinates: 52.37424, 4.90030

Step 4: Walk to Dam Square
Continue southwest along Zeedijk, which becomes Geldersekade.

Turn right (northwest) on Nieuwendijk or Warmoesstraat.

Walk ~500 meters to Dam Square.

Stop near the National Monument in the center of the square.

Stop Coordinates: 52.37317, 4.89361

Step 5: Walk to Begijnhof
Exit Dam Square via Kalverstraat (southwest corner of the square).

Walk ~300 meters to Spui Square.

Enter Begijnhof through the passage at Spui 30.

Stop Coordinates (courtyard center): 52.36840, 4.88986

Continue west, crossing Herengracht, Keizersgracht, Prinsengracht.

Stop Coordinates (Reestraat bridge over Prinsengracht): 52.36880, 4.88496

Step 7: Walk to Jordaan, Westerkerk & Anne Frank House
From Reestraat bridge, turn right (north) along Prinsengracht.

Walk ~300 meters to Westerkerk tower and Anne Frank House at no. 263.

Stop Coordinates (Anne Frank House): 52.37522, 4.88357

Final Stop Coordinates (Westerkerk): 52.37557, 4.88390
